How to return to THIS page from an "open Newsletter"

The Newsletter you selected is opened in your browser (i.e., Google Chrome, Foxfire, etc.). In the upper left corner of the browser widow "frame" you will see symboles " <     > ". To return to the Newsletter webpage move your cursor to the " < " symbol and left click. That should return you to the Newsletter webpage.
