MEMBERSHIPS

The 31st Infantry Regiment Association has only "Lifetime" memberships. You pay dues one time, and never need to worry about annual dues payings again. In recognition of the various stages that we all transition through in our lives, the level of lifetime dues is graduated according to age cohorts and active duty and Associate members (i.e., for those who didn't serve but wish to associate with us; e.g., family members of former Polar Bears, etc.) the dues are $25; for our members ages 49 and under, $60; for ages 50-59 $45; for ages 60 and over, $35.
It is our intent that the dues amount not be a barrier to entry to the Association, so they are set to be affordable. However, the dues schedule may change from time to time to adjust for inflation.
